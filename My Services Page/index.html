About

From Operations to
n8n Automation

Jeremiah Ragay built his foundation inside real business operations, supporting executives, managing pipelines, and working closely with CRM systems and client workflows. Being deeply involved in execution exposed a recurring issue: most bottlenecks aren't caused by people, they're caused by inefficient systems. Manual follow-ups, scattered data, and disconnected tools quietly slow growth and create unnecessary friction.

With a background in Information Technology, he shifted from managing processes to rebuilding them using n8n. Today, he designs structured automation systems that eliminate repetitive work, connect business tools into a unified workflow, and transform day-to-day operations into scalable, reliable infrastructure.
